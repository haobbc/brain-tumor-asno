## 5. vs-cbtrus — International comparison (5 steps · ~55s)

**Info pool** (denominator-matched after methodological calibration):
- **Primary CNS denominator** for our cohort = 3,309 − 619 metastatic = **n = 2,690** (to match CBTRUS denominator)
- CBTRUS meningioma 39.7% (all primary CNS) vs ours **26.6%** (715/2,690, all grades) —— Ostrom 2022 / cohort recalc
- CBTRUS GBM 14.2% (all primary CNS) vs ours **20.2%** (543/2,690) — **higher**, direction reversed from article §Discussion which used mismatched denominators
- CBTRUS GBM = 50.1% of malignant cases —— Ostrom 2022 (context only, different denominator)
- Pituitary 16.5% of all surgeries · **20.3%** of primary CNS · within Molitch community range 10-22% —— article §Discussion / Molitch 2017
- Referral bias interpretation: tertiary center concentrates complex malignant + pituitary; conservatively managed meningioma stays in community —— article §Discussion
