## 5. vs-cbtrus — 國際比較（5 steps · ~55s）

**資訊池**（方法校準後的 denominator-matched comparison）：
- **Primary CNS denominator** for our cohort = 3,309 − 619 metastatic = **n = 2,690**（to match CBTRUS denominator）
- CBTRUS meningioma 39.7%（all primary CNS）vs ours **26.6%**（715/2,690, all grades）—— Ostrom 2022 / cohort recalc
- CBTRUS GBM 14.2%（all primary CNS）vs ours **20.2%**（543/2,690）— **higher**，方向與 article §Discussion 使用 mismatched denominators 時相反
- CBTRUS GBM = 50.1% of malignant cases —— Ostrom 2022（context only, different denominator）
- Pituitary 16.5% of all surgeries · **20.3%** of primary CNS · within Molitch community range 10–22% —— article §Discussion / Molitch 2017
- Referral bias interpretation：tertiary center concentrates complex malignant + pituitary；conservatively managed meningioma stays in community —— article §Discussion
- 視覺：side-by-side comparison cards, CBTRUS vs CGMH, with denominator label「n = 2,690 primary CNS」

**開發計畫**：
- step 31 (~3s) — section pivot：「Now compare to CBTRUS」+ denominator-matching 提醒
- step 32 (~15s) — comparison panel：meningioma — CBTRUS 39.7%（all primary CNS）vs CGMH 26.6%（715/2,690 primary CNS surgeries）+ verdict「underrepresented」
- step 33 (~14s) — comparison：GBM — CBTRUS 14.2%（all primary CNS）vs CGMH 20.2%（543/2,690）+ verdict「overrepresented」
- step 34 (~10s) — referral-bias funnel：complex malignant + pituitary concentrate；conservatively managed meningioma stays in community
- step 35 (~13s) — comparison：pituitary — CGMH 16.5% of all surgeries / 20.3% of primary CNS，落在 Molitch 10–22% community range；解讀為 expertise concentration

**節錄**：
> CBTRUS reports meningioma at 39.7% of all primary CNS tumors. After denominator matching, we see 26.6% of primary CNS surgeries. Still lower than CBTRUS — many CBTRUS meningiomas are managed conservatively and never reach an operating room.
